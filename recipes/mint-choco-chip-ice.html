Description

The recipe yields 1 tub of ice cream, it is perfect for a family or for yourself.

The recipe is very flexible, you can change or leave out certain ingredients as you would like.

Ingredients

Mint Ice Cream üåø

  • 100g cashew nuts
  • 20g coconut oil
  • 80g vegetable oil (less flavored oil is recommended)
  • 100g maple syrup
  • 100g cane sugar
  • 600g soy milk (substitute: your choice of milk)
  • 15g mint leaves (washed)
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract

Chocolate Chips üç´

  • Optionally: Substitute the below instead with store-bought chocolate bars or vegan chocolate chips
  • 30g cacao butter
  • 30g cacao powder
  • 15g maple syrup

Steps

  1. Add your cashew nuts, coconut oil, vegetable oil, cane sugar, maple syrup, milk, and mint leaves in the blender.
  2. Blend all your ingredients in the blender (your mixture should look frothy).
  3. Add your vanilla extract in the mixture.
  4. Get a pan and add your cacao butter, cacao powder, and maple syrup.
  5. Turn the heat on and melt and stir the melted chocolate together.
  6. Turn the ice cream machine on and pour the mixture into the ice cream machine.
  7. Let the machine run for 10 minutes or so until the mixture thickens.
  8. Pour in your melted chocolate gradually as the machine turns.
  9. Take out the ice cream mixture using a spatula and put it in a container.
  10. Put the ice cream in the freezer for about 6 hours.
  11. Serve the ice cream.
