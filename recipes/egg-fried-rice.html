Uncle Roger's Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This is Uncle Roger's egg fried rice recipe which yields 4 servings of egg fried rice. No vegetables because he says, "vegetables taste like sad." ðŸ˜”

Ingredients

  • At least 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 shallot
  • 1 full egg and 1 egg yolk
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 red chili
  • 4 cups "chicken rice" (rice cooked with chicken stock, ginger, and garlic left overnight)
  • About 2 tbsp. Kikkoman Soy Sauce
  • About 2 tbsp. Aster Pure 100% Sesame Oil
  • About 1/2 tbsp. MSG Sasa (Monosodium Glutamate; Indonesian brand)
  • 2 tbsp. peanut oil (substitutes: pork lard or vegetable oil)
  • Optional: white pepper

Prep-work

  1. Prepare the chicken rice the day before you want to make egg fried rice, leave it overnight in the fridge: rice made from chicken stock, ginger, and garlic.
  2. Have all of your ingredients ready (mise en place).
  3. Smash or chop the garlic cloves finely.
  4. Make some vertical cuts on the shallot and then slice the shallot from the middle. Chop finely.
  5. Whisk the egg with chopsticks.
  6. Chop the spring onion, chop away the excess and save it for other cooking.

Steps

  1. First, coat the wok with oil. Once the oil starts smoking, throw away the excess oil.
  2. Spread the oil nicely around the wok and then add the garlic and shallot.
  3. Fry the garlic and shallot for a bit until it's fragrant.
  4. Next, add the egg and stir it around.
  5. Fry the egg, when the egg is almost all solid and fluffy, add in the rice. Stir.
  6. Add in two swirls of oil around the wok.
  7. Add in your soy sauce, coat the rice.
  8. Add in your sesame oil, flatten the rice to avoid clumping. Stir.
  9. Sprinkle in your MSG over the rice.
  10. Add in your garnishes, the spring onion and chili.
  11. Stir everything around and toss the wok.
  12. Serve on a plate.
